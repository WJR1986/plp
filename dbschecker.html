Do I Need to Disclose My Criminal Record?

1. Was your conviction for a serious offence? (e.g., violent crime, sexual offence)

2. Has the conviction been spent under the Rehabilitation of Offenders Act?

3. How long has it been since your conviction was completed?

4. Are you applying for a job that requires a DBS check?

5. Does the job require a Standard or Enhanced DBS check?

6. Does the job involve working with vulnerable groups (children or adults in care)? What are vulnerable groups?

Vulnerable groups include children, the elderly, and people with disabilities who may need extra protection or care.
